Large-scale CO surveys

Large-scale surveys in CO isotopic lines not only provide the information on the precise content and distribution of molecular gas in Milky Way, but also reveal the kinematics of molecular clouds. Now I am a member of the Milky Way Imaging Scroll Painting (MWISP) project.

Large shells at the Outer arm, unveiled by MWISP

Gong et al. (2016), A&A, 574, A56

Spectral line surveys

Spectral line surveys are the only way to obtain a complete unbiased view of the molecular inventory. The resulting data will provide an useful reference database for astrochemists and observers. I was a member of the team working on the 1.3 cm molecular line survey toward prototypical astronomical sources.

A 1.3 cm line survey toward IRC +10216

Gong et al. (2015), A&A, 574, A56

Complex organic interstellar molecules

Complex organic molecules contain 6 or more atoms which include the element carbon. So far, they are only detected in denser sources. Studying these molecules will give us a clue about the origin of life on the earth.
